Family Relationships

Vocabulary Level A2 15 min

Family is often the first topic we talk about when meeting new people. Knowing how to describe your family members and relationships is essential for basic conversation in English.

In this activity, you will learn the vocabulary for immediate family (parents, siblings) and extended family (grandparents, cousins, aunts, and uncles). You will also practise how to describe relationships using a family tree.

Mastering these words will help you share your personal story and understand others when they talk about their lives.

Learning Objective

You will be able to identify and name family members and describe relationships.

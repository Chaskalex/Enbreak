Spelling Names

Vocabulary Level A1 15 min

Spelling is a crucial skill in English. Unlike some languages where words are read exactly as they are written, English spelling can be tricky.

In professional and social situations, you will often need to spell your name, email address, or street name over the phone. Mastering the alphabet sounds is the first step to clear communication.

In this activity, you will start by reviewing the alphabet and special symbols. Then, you will put your skills to the test with practical exercises designed to help you communicate with confidence.

Learning Objective

You will be able to spell names and understand spelled words correctly.

Tip
  • It is very common to say "that's" before starting to spell. Example: "My name is Trisha, that's T-R-I-S-H-A, Trisha."
  • When two letters are repeated, people say "double + letter". Example: "Miller, M-I-double L-E-R."
